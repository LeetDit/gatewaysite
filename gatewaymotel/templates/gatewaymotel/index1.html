Welcome


Welcome! Gateway motel is clean, quiet and affordable motel located on the junction of highway #9 and highway #5 in town of Canora, SK.

With 3 lakes and 2 provincial parks located close to the motel, Gateway Motel is truly a place to enjoy your stay in Canora.

Our 14 guest rooms offer; 1 double bed, 2 double beds, 1 queen bed (smoking room available).

Each of our room features flat-screen TV, hardwood flooring, microwave, fridge, coffeemaker, and high-speed Wi-Fi.

Our rooms have been refurbished to a high standard of comfort and modern. With a huge parking space in front of your room doors,

We welcome large sized vehicles, such as company trucks, semi-trucks, or RVs.

Barbeque is available upon request for customers to cook!

We offer weekly/monthly rates for customers looking to stay for a long duration. We welcome wedding, funeral or reunion guest for multiple rooms.

Our green and fresh front yard is ideal for children and pets to play around on(we are pets friendly place).

The warm comfort from the reception will make you feel like you are home!
